Positions

What I'm
Fighting For

  1. 01

    Energy Freedom

    SRP has the potential to save you money, conserve water, improve air quality, and support your right to generate clean energy from your own home.

  2. 02

    Voter Awareness

    Many people don't know they can vote in SRP elections, even if they get power from APS. Ken is fighting to increase participation.

  3. 03

    Board Accountability

    SRP Board decisions impact every customer's rates and water. Those decisions should be transparent and serve the public interest.

  4. 04

    Local Economy

    Supporting local businesses and farmers. Infrastructure investments should benefit Arizona communities, not just corporate shareholders.
