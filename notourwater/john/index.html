The Platform

What I'm
fighting for.

  1. 01

    Solar Freedom

    Arizona gets 299 sunny days a year. Customers should have the right to generate their own clean energy without being penalized by high SRP solar fees. I oppose the fees that punish homeowners for going solar.

  2. 02

    Water Conservation

    We live in a desert. Every drop matters. I prioritize long-term water sustainability over short-term corporate gains and demand full accountability for data center water consumption.

  3. 03

    Fair Cost Sharing

    Large industrial users should pay their proportional share of infrastructure costs. The burden of grid expansion and maintenance should not be shifted to residential customers subsidizing billion-dollar corporations.

  4. 04

    Affordable Energy

    Arizona families should never have to choose between cooling their homes and paying their bills. I oppose rate increases that squeeze working households while corporate users get preferential pricing.

"I'm fed up with rising energy costs and SRP's hostility toward solar. Our neighbors deserve better."
— John Travise, candidate for SRP Council, District 6
